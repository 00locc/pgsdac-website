Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

The Phoenix Ghanaian Seventh-day Adventist Church is a vibrant faith community celebrating our rich Ghanaian heritage while building a home in the heart of Phoenix, Arizona. We are part of the global Seventh-day Adventist Church, a movement united by the hope of Christ's soon return.

Our mission is to make disciples of all people — proclaiming the everlasting gospel, strengthening families, and serving our community with compassion. We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, and we gather every Saturday — the biblical Sabbath — to worship, study, and fellowship together.

Whether you are new to faith or have walked with God for decades, you are welcome here. Our doors are open to all who seek truth, community, and the love of God.
