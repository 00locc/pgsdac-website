Our Ministries

There is a place for everyone to serve, grow, and connect within our church family.

👶

Children's Ministry

Nurturing young hearts in the love and knowledge of God. We provide a safe, fun, and spiritually enriching environment for children to grow in faith.

🎵

Music Ministry

Praising God through joyful and uplifting worship. Our choir and musicians lead the congregation in powerful Sabbath worship each week.

👩‍👧

Women's Ministry

Empowering women to grow spiritually, support one another, and serve boldly in the church and community.

👨‍💼

Men's Ministry

Building men of integrity, faith, and purpose. We equip men to lead their families and communities with the love of Christ.

🌍

Community Outreach

Serving Phoenix with compassion and practical help. From food drives to health screenings, we are the hands and feet of Jesus.

📚

Youth Ministry

Inspiring the next generation to live for Christ. Our youth programs offer mentorship, fellowship, and spiritual growth activities.

🏥

Health Ministry

Promoting wholistic wellness in body, mind, and spirit — a cornerstone of Adventist living and community service.

📖

Prayer Ministry

Interceding for our church, community, and world. We believe in the power of prayer and gather regularly to seek God together.

🎓

Sabbath School

Deep Bible study for all ages every Saturday morning at 9:30 AM. Grow in the Word alongside your church family.
