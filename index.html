Our Mission

Come and Fellowship
With Us

We are a vibrant Ghanaian Seventh-Day Adventist community in the heart of Phoenix, Arizona. We welcome all who seek to grow in faith, find community, and serve others with the love of Christ.

Whether you're new to the faith or a lifelong believer, there is a place for you here. Come as you are — you are loved.
